Players use their resources to build cities, train armies, construct buildings, and upgrade their buildings, cities, and armies.

### Types of Resources

- Gold
- Wood
- Ore
- Stone

### Collecting Resources

Resources are primarly collected and produced in players' cities by the cities' buildings. Other means of acquiring resources include taxing vassals, mining resource tiles, and winning battles.
